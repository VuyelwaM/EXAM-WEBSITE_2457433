About Me

In the enchanting world that is my life, I, Vuyelwa, emerged under the nurturing care of my beloved grandmothers. As if destiny had a mischievous sense of humor, it was within their comforting embrace that my love affair with tea began. The aroma of their wisdom, mixed with the gentle notes of chamomile and earl grey, danced around our cozy kitchen. And oh, the fun fact that brings a smile to my face: both of my names were gifted to me by these remarkable women. With every sip, I raise my teacup to honor their legacy, feeling their love and guidance flowing through my veins. Tea, my dear friend, is not just a beverage; it's a magical thread that connects generations of warmth and joy.

Designers Favourite

In the realm of cherished memories, one game reigns supreme: God of War. It was a sacred bond shared between my father and me, a thrilling saga of epic battles and heartfelt moments. But fate took an unexpected turn when my PS mysteriously vanished. The mystery remains unsolved, leaving behind bittersweet nostalgia.

Enter the sweet and addictive realm of Candy Crush, a digital delight that captures my undivided attention. There's an inexplicable magic to this game, especially when I'm armed with Wi-Fi. With each sugary swipe, I unlock a world of delectable combos that tempt my taste buds and challenge my puzzle-solving skills. Whether it's a quick break or a marathon session.

Cleo Sol, a cherished artist who feels like the older sister I never had. Her music transcends melodies and touches the depths of the soul. Through her wise and comforting lyrics, she weaves a tapestry of emotions, offering solace and understanding. With every song, Cleo Sol becomes a guiding light in the symphony of life.

Alfredo pasta, the delicious dish that stirs memories of my inseparable best friend of 6 years. Sharing not only laughter and secrets but also culinary preferences. What she eats, I eat, and that includes the delectable Alfredo pasta masterpiece crafted at Panarottis. It's a flavorful symphony that symbolizes our friendship, as we savor each creamy bite and toast to the unbreakable connection we share.

When the divine architect shaped the concept of friendship, it was a stroke of genius. Amidst life's uncertainties and injustices, my friends emerged as the most precious gifts. Their presence, like a warm embrace, brings light and laughter to my world. And oh, those girls' night outs! They're like magical portals where worries fade, and pure joy takes center stage.

In the realm of vibrant celebrations, my 21st birthday emerged wrapped in a rosy hue: Pink! From the cake that tickled my taste buds to the bedding that cradles my dreams, everything embraced the whimsical charm of pink. Even the walls, dressed in pink wallpaper. Oh, how I adore this color! It dances with joy and paints my world in shades of happiness
